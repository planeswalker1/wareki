What is this?

Although most people in Japan use, or at least are familiar with the Western calendar (seireki: 西暦), the traditional calendar (wareki: 和暦) is still used for official documents such as a resume and driver's license. The traditional Japanese calendar is based on the reign period of the emperor. Each time a different emperor begins to rule, a new counting of the years begins and the period acquires a new name. This calculator convert seireki to wareki.
